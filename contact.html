Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a quote after submitting the form?
We typically respond within 12 hours during business days (Monday-Friday). For weekend submissions, you'll receive a response by Monday morning. Urgent requests are prioritized - please indicate if your project is time-sensitive.
What information should I include in my project description?
Please include: 1) Subject/topic of your project, 2) Specific requirements or instructions, 3) Page/slide count or duration (for videos), 4) Deadline, 5) Any reference materials or examples, 6) Preferred file formats, 7) Any grading rubrics or evaluation criteria you have.
Do you offer discounts for bulk orders or returning customers?
Yes! We offer a 10% discount for returning customers and 15% off for bulk orders (3+ projects). Student referrals also receive special rates. Mention any previous work or referrals when submitting your request.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept PayPal, bank transfers (JMD or USD), and local Jamaican payment methods. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon completion, though this can be adjusted for established clients or smaller projects.
Can I request revisions if I'm not satisfied with the work?
Absolutely! Each pricing tier includes a specific number of revisions (Basic: 1, Standard: 2, Premium: Unlimited). We work with you until you're completely satisfied with the final product. Additional revisions beyond the included amount may incur a small fee.
